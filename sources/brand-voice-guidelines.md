## Voice Attributes

- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Helpful** but not patronising
- **Clear** but not simplistic
- **Warm** but not unprofessional

## Tone Variations by Channel

- Social media: conversational, punchy
- Email: personal, direct
- Website: clear, benefit-focused
- Formal comms: professional, measured
