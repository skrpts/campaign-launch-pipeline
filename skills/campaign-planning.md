## Capability

Develops multi-channel campaign plans covering objectives, target audiences, channels, timeline, budget allocation, and success metrics.

## When to Use

- Launching new products or features
- Seasonal or event-driven campaigns
- Brand awareness initiatives
- Lead generation programmes

## Inputs

Business objectives, target audience, budget, timeline, available channels

## Outputs

Campaign brief with: objectives, audience segments, channel mix, content calendar, budget breakdown, KPIs
