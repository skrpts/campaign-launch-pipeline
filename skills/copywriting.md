## Capability

Produces compelling copy tailored to specific channels (web, email, social, print) while maintaining brand voice and driving action.

## When to Use

- Writing landing page copy
- Drafting email campaigns
- Creating social media posts
- Writing product descriptions

## Inputs

Brief (audience, channel, goal, tone), brand guidelines, key messages

## Outputs

Polished copy with headline options, body text, and call-to-action variants
