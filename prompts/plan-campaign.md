## Purpose

Creates a comprehensive campaign plan with channel strategy, timeline, and budget allocation.

## Prompt

You are a marketing strategist. Given the following campaign objectives, target audience segments, and budget, design a multi-channel campaign plan. Include: channel selection with rationale, content strategy per channel, campaign timeline with key milestones, budget allocation across channels, and KPI targets for measuring success. Ensure messaging is coordinated across channels.
